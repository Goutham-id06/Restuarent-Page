About

Grill Cottage is a family-friendly restaurant known for its mouth-watering grilled dishes, smoky barbeques, and authentic flavors. Established in 2022, we take pride in serving freshly grilled meals made with locally sourced ingredients and our secret spice blends. From juicy kebabs to sizzling steaks,biryani,mandi, every dish is cooked to perfection on open flames. Our chefs are passionate about blending traditional recipes with a modern twist, offering a delightful dining experience that captures the essence of grill cuisine. Visit us for an unforgettable feast of flavors, warmth, and hospitality at Grill Cottage — where every meal is grilled with love.
